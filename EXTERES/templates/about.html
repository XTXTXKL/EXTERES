We built EXTERES to solve the single greatest challenge of the digital age: maintaining deep, meaningful concentration.

Our Founding Story

The Problem We Set Out to Solve

EXTERES was born from frustration. Our founders, veterans of high-stress industries, recognized that existing productivity tools merely managed tasks—they didn't address the root cause of inefficiency: **distraction**. We found ourselves spending more time organizing work than doing it.

This led to a simple, powerful vision: create the first platform that proactively safeguards your focus, using smart technology to eliminate friction between intent and execution.

Innovation Through Intelligence

We believe true productivity is not about working harder, but **smarter**. Our proprietary AI algorithms analyze your workflow, identify personalized optimal work windows, and dynamically prioritize your task queue. This intelligence is woven into every feature, from Deep Focus Sessions to Intelligent Reminders.

Our commitment is to transparency, utility, and user control. EXTERES works for you, giving you back the time and mental energy you deserve.
